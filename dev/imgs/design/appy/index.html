Recipe

User can choose to view the recipe through our quality videos or the written instructions below. Additionally for certain steps there are AR features to guide the user, as all recipes come with an ingredients list that noods helps you face!

AR

Fridge AR

Using the camera function, you may use our Fridge AR. This feature is beneficial when preparing the ingredients necessary for your ramen, because not only will Naruto check what ingredients are avaialble in your fridge, but he will also appear next to the ingredients you do have, saving you the hassle of looking for them. Any ingredients you do not have will be automatically added into your shopping list.

AR

Measurement AR

It's impossible for everyone to have all the measuring tools cooking may require, and even if you do have them, measuring everything precisely would be a hassle, therefore we have a measurement tool built into the app. During food preperations and cooking, Naruto will appear and show you exactly where to cut, dice, etc. along, always leaving you with perfect recipe!
